# Fund Performance Analysis

## Top 5-Year Return Funds

The highest-performing schemes over a five-year horizon were dominated by small-cap funds.

Top Performer:
- ABSL Small Cap Fund – Regular Growth

Insight:
Small-cap funds delivered superior long-term returns but are associated with higher volatility and risk.

---

## Alpha Analysis

Top Performer:
- HDFC Short Term Debt Fund – Regular Growth

Insight:
These funds generated excess returns relative to their benchmark performance, indicating effective fund management.

---

## Sharpe Ratio Analysis

Top Performer:
- ICICI Pru Liquid Fund – Regular Growth

Insight:
Liquid and debt-oriented schemes achieved the strongest risk-adjusted returns due to lower volatility and stable performance characteristics.

---

## Business Conclusion

Different fund categories excel under different evaluation metrics:

- Small-cap funds lead long-term returns.
- Debt funds lead Alpha generation.
- Liquid funds lead risk-adjusted performance.

Investors should evaluate funds using multiple performance metrics rather than relying solely on returns.
