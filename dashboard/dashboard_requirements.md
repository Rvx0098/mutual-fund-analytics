# Mutual Fund Analytics Dashboard Requirements

## Dashboard Objective

Provide an executive-level overview of mutual fund performance, investor behavior, SIP growth, and portfolio composition.

---

# Page 1: Executive Summary

## KPI Cards

- Total Funds
- Total Fund Houses
- Total Investors
- Total Transactions
- Total Investment Amount
- Latest SIP Inflow
- Average Expense Ratio
- Average AUM

## Charts

- Top 10 Funds by AUM
- SIP Growth Trend

## Business Questions

- How large is the mutual fund ecosystem?
- How fast is SIP adoption growing?
- Which schemes dominate assets?
